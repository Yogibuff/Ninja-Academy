FAQ's and Academy Details


FAQ Topic:1 Required Gear

We require that you purchase and be responsible for your own practice sword at Ninja Academy. These cost under $100 and are available at a discount from our friends at from ninjagear.com, who also have a great selection of practice gear and clothes that are fitting for quick movement and martial arts combat. We recommend you purchase at least 3 sparring outfits from ama.com. Additionally be sure to bring casual clothing for meals and daily meditation or relaxation time. If you are intermediate to advanced level, please include boxing gloves and a mouthguard.

FAQ Topic:2 Teamwork Training

Teamwork is a huge part of being a ninja; although many people think of ninjas as rogues or solo-mission officianados. Teamwork will be the key to using the strengths of your comrade ninjas who may be better or weaker but are part of the team and you have to learn to deal with their percieved strengths and weaknesses without letting your ego overcome battle strategy and the ninja code. Those who break the ninja code are disciplined, but abandoning a teammate is even worse.

FAQ Topic:3 Weapons Training

Weapons such as swords, specifically the kitana, will be emphasized at the intermediate level. At the beginner level you will learn to use traditional ninja kunaii (a small dagger that can be thrown or used like a knife. If you are lacking in weapons skills, hand-to-hand combat is for you! Do not be discouraged if you are good at only one, as this is very common. If you feel you are proficient but not good at either, you can focus on the art of dodging and being fast, nimble, and elusive. Ninjas do not wear much protection, so this is an honorable skill to develop.
